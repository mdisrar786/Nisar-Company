About Us

Leading Automotive Equipment Manufacturer

AutoTech Solutions has been at the forefront of automotive service equipment innovation for over 25 years. We specialize in designing and manufacturing high-performance tools and machinery for professional automotive workshops.

Our product range includes two-post lifts, wheel alignment systems, tire changers, wheel balancers, and air compressors that meet the highest industry standards. We're committed to providing reliable, efficient, and safe equipment that helps our customers maximize productivity and profitability.

With a global distribution network and dedicated technical support teams, we ensure that our customers receive the best service and assistance whenever they need it.
